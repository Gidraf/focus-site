Focus Financial and Management Consultants Ltd

Is a financial and management consulting firm incorporated in 2015 with the aim of providing professional, advisory and training services in the areas of Accounting, Finance, Audit, Tax, Human Resources, Strategic Management, Business Development

Our Vision

To be the impeccable provider of Financial & Management Solutions in Kenya and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide Financial & management consultancy to individuals, Small Medium Enterprises (SME’s) and corporate bodies in order to enhance the achievability of their set goals as well as promoting best practices that encompass around growth & sustainability of the business in the dynamic operating environment.

Product and Services

Training

Training is a strategic must for any organization to survive today’s turbulent business environment. We develop our trainings to help you adapt and succeed in this dynamic business environment. The core objective of our training is to equip managers and stakeholders at all levels with financial management and business compliance skills that they need to compliment their technical expertise, thereby positioning them to contribute better to the achievement of their organizations goals.
We offer the highest quality facilitator-led courses, produced and delivered to meet the specific learning needs of our clients. No matter the subject or level of expertise required, we provide fully experienced facilitators and custom course materials to match exact client needs. We conduct both external and in-house training programs in customer service, governance, financial management, enterprise risk management, capitalization, taxation, budgeting & finance for non-finance managers, Fraud & internal controls, audit & assurance among others

Financial Management Services

We undertake Financial Management assignments ranging from raising capital, capital restructuring, financial planning, developing financial policies relating to liquidity, internal controls, dividends ,capitalization and process review through to providing on-site interim financial expertise on a day to day basis. We have the expertise and capacity to ensure that our clients are highly satisfied with our pragmatic approach and the outcomes.

Book - Keeping & Accounting Services

Outsourcing your Bookkeeping and Accounting function is a smart way of achieving operational efficiency. By turning over your accounting function to us, you will have more time to focus your resources at your core business while highly trained accountants crunch the numbers for you at a cost much lower than having an internal accounting function. Outsourcing Accounting function saves  you the costs and time of hiring in-house resources while affording you the most current  accounting practice.
Our range of Accounting Services include:-
  • Data capture from original records and recording in the books of accounts
  • Preparation of monthly, quarterly and annual reports
  • Preparation of books for audit purposes
  • Tax Planning and Management
  • Financial Analysis and Management Reports including discussions on financial performance

Strategic , Human Resource and Organization Development Consulting

Organizations spend dedicated time defining their strategy but don’t pay enough attention to getting the strategy implemented. At FFMC, our consulting services are aimed towards providing you with expertise to capture your strategy in a powerful way and align the entire organization towards full implementation driven to its achievement
This entails:
  • Strategy formulation, strategic planning and business planning
  • Assurance , Risk and Forensic Engagements
  • Advising on Competitive and growth strategy
  • Performance Management
  • Change Management
  • Human Resource Audit , Recruitment and Selection
  • Job analysis and evaluation
  • Team building activities
  • Business process re-engineering
